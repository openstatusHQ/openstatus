title: ntfy.sh
description: "How to set up ntfy.sh notifications in OpenStatus to get alerts when your synthetic check fail"
---

[ntfy.sh](https://ntfy.sh/) is an open-source servicer that allows you to receive push notifications on your phone.




Go to the Notifications Page . Select `ntfy.sh` from the list of available integrations.

Enter your ntfy topic (required), your custom server URL, and your bearer token.
