Openstatus now ships a built-in chat assistant. Open `Assistant` from the dashboard sidebar and talk to your workspace in natural language — no MCP client, no Slack, no context-switching.

Every tool call renders inline as a table or change diff so you can see exactly what the assistant is about to do — and every mutation lands in the audit log under your actor.

Read the full write-up on [our blog](/blog/from-clickops-to-chatops).
