You can now restrict access to your status pages by client IP address. Configure a list of allowed IPv4 CIDR ranges, and visitors outside those ranges will be blocked from viewing the page.

This is ideal for internal status pages served only to your office network, VPN, or a known set of partner IPs — no password or email verification required for visitors on approved networks.

Enable it from the status page _Visibility_ settings by selecting the new **IP Restriction** access type and entering one or more comma-separated CIDR ranges (bare IPs are automatically treated as `/32`).

> This feature is available as a paid add-on on the Starter plan and above.
