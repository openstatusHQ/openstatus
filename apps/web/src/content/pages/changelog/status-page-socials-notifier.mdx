When you publish a status report or schedule maintenance, you often want to announce it on social media too — without copy-pasting the same message into each app.

[`statuspage-socials-notifier`](https://github.com/openstatusHQ/statuspage-socials-notifier) is a small open-source service that receives your status page's webhook payload and cross-posts each update to your X (formerly Twitter) and Bluesky accounts.

Add it as a webhook subscriber on your status page, deploy it to Vercel, Fly.io, or Railway, and it does the rest.

Follow the [guide](https://www.openstatus.dev/docs/guides/how-to-deploy-statuspage-socials-notifier) to set it up.
