The uptime bars on your status page can now show **30 or 45 days** of history — pick whichever fits your page from the status page configuration form.

Previously the number of bars was fixed and applied account-wide. It's now a per-page setting stored on the page itself, so different status pages can show different history windows.

The selector lives under **History** in the status page configuration. The default stays at 45 days, so existing pages are untouched.

Check out the [how to configure your status page](https://www.openstatus.dev/docs/guides/how-to-configure-status-page/) guide for more details.
