Migrate from **Atlassian Statuspage**, **Better Stack**, or **Instatus** with our new one-click importers. Your components, incidents, maintenances, subscribers, and monitors are brought over in three steps — select your provider, preview what will be imported, and hit import.

| | Statuspage | Better Stack | Instatus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Status page | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Components & groups | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Incidents | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Maintenances | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Subscribers | Yes (email only) | No (API limitation) | Yes (email only) |
| Monitors | No | Yes | No |

Read the full announcement on our [blog post](/blog/import-from-statuspage-betterstack-instatus) or jump straight to a provider-specific migration guide:

- [Migrate from Atlassian Statuspage](/guides/migrate-from-atlassian-statuspage)
